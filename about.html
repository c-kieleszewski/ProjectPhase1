ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Christopher K and I was born and raised in metro Detroit. I attended High School in 2020 where I continued my education at Oakland University in Rochester, MI. I have a cat named Ollie. I love fishing, traveling, and sports.

I love fishing. My favorite types of fish to target include norther pike, largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, and panfish. My favorite type of fishing is casual lake fishing while targeting anything. I like to spend most of my free time fishing in the summer.

I also love to travel. Some places I have been include california and the Florida Keys. I enjoyed a bit of hiking while being in California and was able to take some incredible pictures. I traveled to Key West, Florida during Christmas and had a fun time with my family.

Finally, I love to watch sports. My favorite sport to watch is definitely football and my favorite team is the Detroit Lions. I have been to some NFL football games and they are exhilarating every time. I also like to watch basketball and baseball. I love the Detroit Pistons and Detroit Tigers.
