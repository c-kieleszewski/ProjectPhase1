DESTINATIONS

This section aims to inform you about various types of places you can travel around the United States and will provide some information about that place as well as some information about some places I have been to.

Los Angeles, California

I have been to Los Angeles and stayed around the area. To start off, I was taken away by the mountains around me as this was the first time I had been near the mountains. Every where I would look I would see mountains. There are endless areas to hike around and outside of Los Angeles which I would recommend to do for anybody. There is a wide range of easy to difficult trails to hike. The drive along the coast near Santa Monica was also very nice aswell as the views from the pier. SoFi stadium in Inglewood in spectatular with its 360 degree view big screen above the field. The size of SoFi stadium is uncomparable to one I've been to before due to its sheer size. The rows of seats must go up 6 or 7 storys into the air.

Zion National Park, Utah

Next up is Zion National Park in Utah. Utah is known for its amazing views and red rock canyons. Visiting Angels Landing in Zion National Park is one of the best things to do here because it is a scenic outlook to some of the best parts of the park. You can also visit The Narrows which is one of the narrowest parts of the park and contains a difficult hike but rewarding scenic views. It is rated the number one thing to do in Zion National Park by TripAdvisor! Finally, you can visit the Emerald Pools in the park which is a short hike that will lead you to beautiful waterfalls. Some people even go swimming in the pools! Zion National Park is the perfect place for anyone to visit such as a young couple looking to start living a little or the solo adventurer looking to thrill seek. One of the must see places in the lower 48!

Glacier National Park, Montana

Glacier National Park in Montana was established in 1910 by president William Taft to allow for the public to enjoy. Glacier National Park features beautiful lakes, mountains, and valleys. One thing that is heavily recommended to do there is St. Mary Lake. St. Mary Lake is the second biggest lake in the park and allows for boating, swimming, and even fishing. Don't let the views around you distract you from your task! The number one thing to do in the park by TripAdvisor is Grinnell Glacier. This is a glacier that allows for 300 acres of breath taking land. It is also known as the heart of the park. Another amazing lake to visit is Avalanche Lake. The hike down to Avalanche Lake is simple and can be done by the whole family. The hike is very rewarding and you can also even swim at the lake after your hike. Glacier National Park in Montana is a fantastic place to visit for the whole family!
