REVIEWS

This section aims to inform you about various types of places you can travel around the United States and will provide some information about that place as well as some information about some places I have been to.

Piper's Pub Gladwin, Michigan

Our family owns a cottage on a lake in Gladwin, Michigan. One of my favorite places to get dinner at is Piper's Pub. They offer mainly american food such as burgers, pizza, and sandwiches. My favorite dish at Piper's is their meat pizza. It is a fairly small restaurant but you do not have to wait usually. The staff is very nice always and I never have anything to complain about. I would recommend Piper's to anyone visiting the Gladwin area in Michigan.

Eggs-N-Things Thousand Oaks, California

During my stay in Thousand Oaks, CA, I was recommended to try Eggs-N-Things which was right down the street. I must say I can't complain about anything during my breakfast here. The complimentary crepe that was served as an appetizer was fantastic. The pancakes were also great. The staff was very nice and tentative. I would definitely recommend this breakfast restaurant to anybody around the Thousand Oaks Area.

Paradise Falls, California

While I was in California, I also visited the Paradise Falls trail. Paradise Falls is a popular photograph spot in Thousand Oaks, California. The trail took around 45 minutes to reach Paradise Falls and was fairly easy. The way up takes around 1 hour as it is pretty steep and more difficult that way. The reward at the end of the trail was worth all the hiking. The falls are very pretty and I would recommend anyone to see them in the area.
